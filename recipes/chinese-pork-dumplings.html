Chinese Pork Dumplings

Description

This Chinese dumplings recipe is from Liana Cafe House in Seattle's International District. They sell these traditional fried pork dumplings from a tiny takeout shop but with this recipe, you can see how to make them at home!

Ingredients

Dipping Sauce

  • soy sauce
  • seasoned rice vinegar
  • Chinese chives, finely chopped
  • sesame seeds
  • chile-garlic sauce

Dumplings

  • ground pork
  • garlic cloves, minced
  • large egg, beaten
  • Chinese chives, finely chopped
  • soy sauce
  • sesame oil
  • fresh ginger, minced
  • dumpling wrappers
  • vegetable oil

Steps

Dipping Sauce

Combine soy sauce, rice vinegar, chives, sesame seeds, and chile-garlic sauce in a small bowl. Set aside.

Dumplings

  1. Mix pork, garlic, egg, chives, soy sauce, sesame oil, and ginger in a large bowl.
  2. Place a dumpling wrapper on a lightly floured work surface and spoon filling in the middle.
  3. Wet the edge with water and crimp together, forming small pleats to seal the dumpling. Repeat to form remaining dumpling.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet. Cook dumplings in pan.
  5. Pour in water. Cover and cook.
  6. Repeat for remaining dumplings, adding more oil and water for each batch.
