Butter Chicken

Description

This butter chicken recipe, or chicken makhani, is one of my favorite Indian dishes. It features a full-flavored sauce that complements the chicken well. It can be made as mild or spicy as you wish by adjusting the cayenne. Serve with basmati rice and naan bread.

Ingredients

  • peanut oil
  • shallot, finely chopped
  • white onion, chopped
  • butter
  • ginger-garlic paste
  • lemon juice
  • garam masala, divided
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • bay leaf
  • tomato puree
  • half-and-half yogurt
  • plain yogurt
  • salt and ground black pepper
  • boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • cayenne pepper
  • cornstarch
  • water

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large saucepan. Saut√© shallot and onion.
  2. Stir in butter, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, garam masala, chili powder, cumin, and bay leaf. Cook and stir. Add tomato sauce. Cook and stir.
  3. Stir in half-and-half yogurt. Reduce heat, simmer, and stir. Season with salt and pepper. Remove from heat and set aside.
  4. Heat oil in a large heavy skillet. Cook chicken.
  5. Reduce heat and season with garam masala and cayenne. Stir in sauce and simmer. Add cooked chicken into sauce and stir.
  6. Dissolve cornstarch into water then mix into sauce. Cook.
